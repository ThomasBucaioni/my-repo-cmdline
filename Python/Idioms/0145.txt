Upset the applecart: spoil or disrupt a plan or arrangement. We had planned to hold a get-together in the evening, but bad weather upset the applecart
Apple of eye: the person of whom one is extremely fond, favorite, loving. She has three children, but her youngest son is the apple of her eye
Apple of discord: something that causes trouble or unhappiness. The right to host the next olympic games has become an apple of discord between the two countries
Ants in pants: be extremely restless. The young students just can't sit still; they must have ants in their pants
Up the ante: increase the demands or risks to obtain better results. The government has upped the ante by refusing to negotiate with the ultras until a ceasefire has been agreed
Run amok (amuck): act in a wild or dangerous manner. When the police arrived, they were confronted with a group of protesters running amuck in the lanes of the old town
All and sundry: everyone. I don't want all and sundry to come to know about our differences
Alpha and omega: both the beginning and the end. He had to learn the alpha and omega of the subject before he could even begin his research
Alive and kicking: lively and active. She hadn't met her younger sister after her marriage and was delighted to see her alive and kicking at a social event last weekend
In the blink of an eye: in an instant. In the blink of an eye, the robber disappeared from the bank with a bag full of cash
