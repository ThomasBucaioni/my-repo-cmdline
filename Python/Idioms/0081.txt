Flesh and blood: this phrase refers to direct family members, people that share a dna, that is, flesh and blood. Of course i care about her well-being, my sister is my flesh and blood after all
High on the hog: someone who is used to luxury. We aren't as wealthy as the jones' to eat so high on the hog
Know the ropes: to know all the ways and means to get something done. She has been working here for 30 years and knows the ropes to getting anything done
Dry run: a rehearsal before the actual performance. Today is only a dry run so we will not be using the actual costumes
Van gogh’s ear for music: van gogh was a painter who cut his ear off and this phrase is a pun intended one referring to being tone deaf. Her singing is so bad she has van gogh's ear for music
Doozy: it is something that is unique. It's a doozy of a painting, she is so creative
A whale of: very big (piece) of something. That birthday party was super fun, all the children had a whale of a time there
Clam up: refusal to talk further or reply. She clams up every time i walk in, it is worrisome
Fish for a compliment: it refers to an attempt to make someone say something nice to you or about you. When the student said that he was still not confident of the subject after a score of 80 on 100 in the test, the teacher knew that he was only fishing for a compliment
Turn turtle: it refers to something turning upside down. The automobile turned turtle on the highway when the storm got too strong
